Learning Goals

When students finish this course, they will be able to:

  1. Explain the definitions of simple interest and compound interest;
  2. Identify the formulas of simple and compound interest and interpret the meaning of each variable;
  3. Apply the formulas to compute simple and compound interest in practical contexts;
  4. Explain the differences between simple and compound interest.
